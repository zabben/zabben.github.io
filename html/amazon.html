Amazon

As their name suggests, the Amazon's natural habitat is around the Amazon river in South America. There are over 30 different kinds of Amazon parrots, the vast majority of these are bright green with touches of yellow. The differences lie mostly in the colour of their heads and their beaks.
Unlike some pet birds that don't mind being by themselves or only their human companion, Amazon parrots prefer another Amazon parrot around them; if you think about getting an Amazon parrot, you should get at least two! In captivity, Amazon parrots tend to pick up the ability to talk quite quickly; more so than any other parrot, they are known for their speech clarity.

Amazon parrots are chewers and gnawers. Therefore its owner should take care and get them toys that are safe and destructible. This might cost you a bit more than with other parrots, but you don't want a bored Amazon in your house.
