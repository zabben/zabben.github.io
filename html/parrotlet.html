Parrotlet

Parrotlets are small, tiny, cute and feisty. What's most pertaining to them is how some of the subspecies fail to understand how tiny they really are, which in the wild can often lead to unpleasant consequences. In captivity, this doesn't really cause that much trouble (maybe it will if you have other pets in the house), but they remain energetic little adventurers with a very strong will of their own. It is therefor very important to let them know that you are the 'leader of the flock' and not them.

Maybe more so than most other pet birds, parrotlets really need their time outside of the cage where they can socialize and explore. They're gnawers and biters, so try and keep them away from wires and cables.
