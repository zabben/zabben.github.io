Ara

The macaw is one of the prettiest animals on the planet, let alone pets. They come in beautiful ranges of colours, from red to blue and nearly every colour of the rainbow in between. However, while the macaw is often a cuddly and sweet pet, they are also strong and can react fiercely when the situation is called upon. Their beaks are extremely powerful; the Hyacinth macaw for instance can break a broomstick in half with little to no effort.
Having a macaw for a pet is not easy. They require your constant attention, love and care. Without it, they can resort to plucking and shrieking. Only consider getting a macaw if you are dedicated and can afford to put your precious time into it. The love you get in return is well worth your while: macaws are generally very cuddly and loving animals.

Macaws range in size from 30 cm up to 100 cm (the Hyacinth macaw, largest of all the parrots). Their life span ranges from 50 years to 80 years. So as with all pets, but especially with these long living ones: make sure you're up to dedicating your time to these animals for many many years!
