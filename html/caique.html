Caique

Caiques are clowns. They are playful little creatures that like to swing back and forth on one foot, lie on their backs to fight an imaginary foe (or each other!) and turn everything they see in their toy. Be careful what you leave lying around the house, because once a caique see it, it's theirs. There is never a dull moment for those with caiques.
They are not the best of flyers, they mostly even prefer jumping, hopping and climbing as their mode of transportation.

Getting a pet bird means making your house bird-proof, especially when you're getting a caique. Electrical cords, lamps, books, your own food, kitchen supplies; nothing is particularly safe. Get rid of those, hide them from your caique and give them all the toys they need (which they really do!).
