Valkparkiet

If you're looking for a sweet and friendly bird, the cockatiel might just be the pet bird for you. While being explorers, they also like to just sit on your shoulder watching your every move. Cockatiels come only second as world's most kept pet bird (after the budgies), while their native habitat is in warm Australia.

Like the cockatoo, the most distinctive feature of the cockatiel is its crest. It actually expresses their feelings: a vertical crest means an excited cockatiel, a skewed or oblique crest means a relaxed cockatiel and a flat crest means an angry cockatiel.
Cockatiels scare easily and can suffer from night terrors. When the lights are out in its sleeping place, they can seemingly randomly start to flap like crazy, even up to the point where they end up hurting themselves. Having a light on might help, but also getting a white noise machine.
