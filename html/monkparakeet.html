Monk parakeet

The monk parakeet (or quaker parrot) haven't been around as pets for a very long time, it has only become more popular during the last couple of decades. It is comparable size-wise with the cockatiel, even gaining in popularity compared to them since some argue it's even easier to teach a monk parakeet to talk. They're much more active than most cockatiels though, they like to fly around stealing your stuff to make their own little nest.

Do not be alarmed when your monk parakeet starts to quickly bob its head, that's normal although it might seem slightly disturbing at first. It's their way of being comfortable. Monk parakeets appreciate close interaction and can become depressed if you leave it alone for too long, so consider getting a pair if you like them, but never neglect them since that might cause problematic behaviour.
