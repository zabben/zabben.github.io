About us

Hi! Our little family currently consists of twelve members: there is Persian cat Kokoshi, Hermann's tortoise Gaia, cockatiels Bijou and Satine, kakarikis Ono and Pixie, budgies Kiki and Mi, African grey Miles, hybrid harlequin macaw Julie and the humans Deborah and Rik. There is never a dull moment in our house, and we hope that we can share our experiences with you.
Although our animals are kept in captivity, we strive to give them as much freedom as we can. We take walks and trips with our parrots (who are with us in the living room when we are home) and the small birds have a big room all for themselves. We chose these animals, they didn't pick us, so we feel it as our responsibility to give them the best lives that we can offer them.
Enjoy the website! And feel free to contact us here.
