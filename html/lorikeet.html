Lorikeet

Like the macaw, the lorikeet is one of the most colourful parrot. Like the conure, the lorikeet can become possesive towards their favorite person. Like the caique, the lorikeet is incredibly playful. Like the Amazon, they prefer to come in pairs.But the lorikeet is still one-of-a-kind. Their funny, nosy, sweet, make funny little noises and are messy in their own little way (their feces are quite unique, it's runny and sticky because their diet is primarely based on liquids).

Because lorikeets are adventurers, you have to make sure they have enough space in the living quarters. They like to be on the move, to jump, to fly and to climb, so make sure you accomodate! They require a lot of you attention, think 3 or 4 hours of quality alone time with your bird per day, especially the first few years. They have a life-span of around 30 years, so it is well worth your time and investment.
