Kakariki

Kakarikis are small parrots from New Zealand, hence their alternative name New Zealand parakeets (kakariki means small parrot in Māori). They make for fun and active little pets, who really need their space for their climbing and gymnastic adventures, because adventurous they really are. Once they got the opportunity, they will go about and investigate their surroundings. When doing that, nothing is safe. They gnaw and they bite, but when you're able to deal with that, Kakarikis make for great little friends in the house. They love to chatter and be around you, but can also have a jolly good time by themselves.

We always support giving your bird as much space to fly as possible, but the Kakariki might be the bird that needs its space the most. If you're not able to give them that you should reconsider getting one (or reconsider how to give them the space they need).
