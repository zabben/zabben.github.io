Budgie

Of all the small birds in the world, the budgerigar (or budgie, or parakeet) is the most commonly kept as companion bird. They even come in third as most kept pet in the world, after cats and dogs! Budgies are great for those who are new to keeping pet birds. They require smallers cages and less space and maintenance than the other birds you find on this site, but that doesn't mean you can give them less attention: like all pet birds, the budgie requires a lot of it.
Male budgies are considered to be in the top five of birds most likely to talk. Other than talking, budgies love to chatter, chirp, twitter and tweet; they're hardly ever silent for more than a couple of minutes. While making many sounds, it's never ear-piercingly loud.

The natural colour of the budgie is green with yellow, but the ones bred in captivity come in wides ranges of blue, violet, white and grey; there are 32 primary mutations reported! Their life spans range between 8 to 20 years, a perfect amount of time to see if having pet birds is right for you.
