Conure

Remember that 90's movie Paulie starring a parrot? That's a conure! A blue-crowned conure to be exact, but there are some 40 different other species and subspecies. The most popular ones as pets are the sun conure, the jenday conure and the green-cheeked conure. The greatest benefit of conures are that they don't necessarily need as much space as some other parrots (which doesn't mean you can get away with caring for them less!).

A conure is a one-person bird, meaning it can bond closely to one person in the household and shun the rest. It can even become hostile towards the ones that are not that one person. This can become problematic, even if it seems like a lot of fun if you are your bird's best friend. Luckily, it's not impossible to keep this from happening. Socialize your bird, make it spend time with all family members and you as the conure's favorite person should refrain from interacting with them as much.
