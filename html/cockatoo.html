Kaketoe

Meet the wacky cockatoo! These large parrots are loud, crazy, but mostly a lot of fun! What they might lack in colourful plumage, they make up for with their personalities. They are intelligent, love to play and love to cuddle.
A cockatoo easily turns into a 'problem' cockatoo if you fail to meet their disproportionate requirements. They tend to resort to plucking and usually start to scream very loudly the second you leave their sight. If you don't have enough time to take care of this beautiful bird, don't get one! It is recommended for someone to be home most of the time. Also, quite randomly, cockatoos do not like children.

There are many different kinds of cockatoos, but the most common ones are the white Moluccan and umbrella cockatoo, the black palm cockatoos and the pink galahs. Their size ranges from 12 to 24 inch, their weight from 300 to 1200 grams.
